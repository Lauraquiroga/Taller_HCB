Harry Potter



What is it?

Harry Potter is a series of seven fantasy novels written by British author J. K. Rowling. The novels narrate the story of a young yet talented wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, all of whom are students at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The main plot of the series consists of Harry's struggle against Lord Voldemort, also referred as The Dark Lord, You-Know-Who or He-Who-Must-Not-Be-Named, a dark wizard who intends to become immortal, overthrow the Ministry of Magic (the wizarding governing body) and subjugate all wizards and Muggles (non-magical people).


The literary series was then adapted into a worldwide successful film series, distributed by Warner Bros, mainly produced by David Jonathan Heyman and staring Daniel Radcliff, Rupert Grint and Emma Watson as the three aforementioned main characters.
